How to use Biodata Maker
  • You can change or remove the God image.
  • You can add a photograph or leave it blank.
  • The mantra text can be edited as per your preference.
  • Fields marked with (*) are mandatory.
  • All other information is optional.
  • Optional fields left empty will not appear in the final biodata.
  • Biodata layout will automatically adjust based on provided information.
